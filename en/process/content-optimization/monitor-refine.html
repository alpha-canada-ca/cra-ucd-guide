Monitor and refine phase

Phase 5 of 5

In the monitor and refine phase, you continually measure the performance of the product launched in the delivery phase to determine how and when to make new user-centred improvements.

To complete this phase, meet the 3 objectives below by following their suggested activities.

Objectives and activities

Monitor product performance

Tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) on a regular basis helps you check if the content continues to meet user and business needs.

  • Product performance monitoring In development

    Regularly monitor and analyze web analytics, search analytics, call driver data, page feedback and periodically conduct usability (UX) testing

Initiate new improvements

Using product performance data analysis will help you identify and prioritize opportunities to improve the content.

  • Addressing minor problems In development

    Identify the minor problems and determine their priority

  • Addressing major problems In development

    Identify the major problems and determine their priority

Transition to product maintenance

Sharing lessons learned and moving to maintenance and continuous improvement ensures the content stays relevant, up to date and accurate after the project closes.

  • Project close-out reporting In development

    Create a retrospective report, document lessons learned, and log project research and data

  • Transition meeting In development

    Have a meeting to close the project and move to maintenance and continuous improvement
