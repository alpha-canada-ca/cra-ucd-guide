Terminology

Using approved terms ensures quality and consistency across our written communication products.

Use these resources to search for terminology that is inclusive and approved for use by the Government of Canada and the CRA.

On this page

  • Government of Canada terminology
  • CRA-specific terminology
  • Inclusive writing

Government of Canada terminology

TERMIUM Plus® is the Government of Canada's terminology and linguistic data bank. It provides access to millions of terms in English, French, Spanish and Portuguese.

Use TERMIUM PLUS to:

  • Find terminology and definitions
  • Look up abbreviations and acronyms
  • Find usage examples
  • Look for equivalents in other languages
  • Check official titles

Refer to: TERMIUM Plus

CRA-specific terminology

Use the CRA glossary to check CRA terminology (includes approved acronyms and initialisms). New terms are added and revised terms are updated regularly.

Refer to: CRA glossary Infozone

The CRA's Language Quality Working Group makes decisions on terminology.

Refer to: Terminology decisions Infozone

Inclusive writing

Consult articles and other resources on the principles and techniques of inclusive writing in English.

The guidelines will help you produce writing that is free of discrimination based on:

  • Sex
  • Gender
  • Sexual orientation
  • Race
  • Ethnicity
  • Disability
  • Any other identity factor

Refer to: Inclusive writing – Guidelines and resources
